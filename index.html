The Beginning

The morning was quiet. The world was wide. I didn’t know where I was going, only that my journey had begun.

Footsteps

I walked with no map, just a feeling in my chest. Every step left something behind. Every step brought me forward.

The Mirror Tree

I saw myself in the still water — not as I was, but as I might become. Sometimes, the world shows us what we’re not ready to see.

The Climb

The hill was steep, and the sky felt heavy. But I kept climbing. Not because I was strong, but because I had to know what was at the top.

The Rain

The rain didn’t ask questions. It just fell. I stood still and let it wash over me — and it felt like starting over.

Memory Shore

The ocean whispered names I didn’t remember. But my heart did. Some places feel like people you once knew.

The Light

The fire cracked and hummed in the quiet night. It didn’t speak, but it made me feel less alone.

The Dream

I walked through a place that made no sense, and yet, it felt familiar. Maybe dreams are just memories from another version of us.

The Return

I came back to where it all began. Not the same as I was… but finally knowing why I left.
